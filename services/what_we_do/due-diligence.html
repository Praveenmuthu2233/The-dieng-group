Transaction Advisory Services

Due Diligence & Vetting

As industries increasingly expand into global markets—particularly in West Africa— it is essential for organizations to conduct comprehensive due diligence and robust vetting procedures before entering into any major business transaction. Proper due diligence safeguards investments, protects stakeholder interests, and ensures transparency throughout the transaction lifecycle.

The Dieng Group partners with experienced transaction advisory professionals who have conducted due diligence for complex and high-value engagements across multiple industries. Our team brings extensive knowledge in evaluating financial, operational, technological, and strategic factors that influence the success of investments and partnerships.

Through our structured approach, we identify potential risks, uncover hidden opportunities, and provide clients with clear insights into the true value of their transactions. Our analysis combines financial expertise, industry intelligence, and strategic advisory to support confident decision-making.

Our Due Diligence Services

Buy-side Due Diligence

We assist investors and acquiring organizations by evaluating potential investment targets. Our analysis focuses on financial performance, operational risks, and long-term growth potential, enabling clients to make informed acquisition decisions.

Sell-side Due Diligence

For organizations preparing for sale or investment, we perform independent evaluations that enhance transparency, address potential buyer concerns, and maximize transaction value.

IT Due Diligence

Our technology specialists review IT infrastructure, cybersecurity posture, and digital capabilities to determine whether systems can support future business growth and integration requirements.

Private Equity Value Creation

We support private equity investors in identifying operational improvements, cost optimization opportunities, and strategic initiatives that drive sustainable value creation.

Post-Transaction Services

Following the completion of transactions, we assist organizations with integration planning, operational restructuring, and performance monitoring to ensure successful outcomes.

Tax Due Diligence & Transaction Structuring

Our tax specialists analyze the tax implications of transactions and design efficient structures that optimize financial outcomes while maintaining regulatory compliance.

Our Approach

The Dieng Group applies a structured methodology to every due diligence engagement. Our process begins with a detailed understanding of the client's objectives, followed by comprehensive data analysis and industry benchmarking. We then provide clear reporting and strategic recommendations to support well-informed decisions.

By combining financial expertise, sector knowledge, and analytical tools, we help organizations navigate complex transactions with confidence and clarity.

Our Passion is to help you grow through reliable overseas investment opportunities.
