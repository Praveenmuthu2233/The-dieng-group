Contracting & Negotiation

We provide strategic support in contract development and negotiations to secure favorable terms, manage risk, and build strong, sustainable partnerships.

Overview

The world is constantly shifting beneath the feet of industries and governmental bodies, and foreign direct investment ought to be as diverse as the populations it serves.

The Dieng Group is constantly engaging U.S. industries to invest in Africa, specifically in West African nations. Our consultancy is persistently working to build dynamic relationships with policymakers in Senegal, The Gambia, and Ethiopia, amongst other countries on the African continent.

Therefore, it is vital to cultivate long-term strategies to attract, secure, and maintain high-quality FDI.

Key Capabilities

Risk Mitigation & Outsourcing

Identifying contract risks and structuring outsourcing strategies to protect value and service continuity.

Complex Agreement Management

Training teams to negotiate, manage, and govern complex contractual agreements effectively.

Cost & Dispute Resolution

Reducing contract costs while resolving disputes through structured negotiation and mediation.

Vendor & Supplier Sourcing

Identifying, evaluating, and selecting qualified vendors aligned with strategic and compliance requirements.

M&A Advisory

Supporting negotiations, due diligence, and contract structuring for mergers and acquisitions.

Planning & Market Analysis

Analyzing markets and planning negotiation strategies to strengthen contracting outcomes.

Post-Signature Management

Managing contracts after signing to ensure compliance, performance, and relationship continuity.
