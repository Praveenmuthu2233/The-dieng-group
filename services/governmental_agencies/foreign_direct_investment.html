Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)

We provide strategic advisory services to attract, structure, and manage foreign investments, ensuring regulatory alignment and sustainable economic impact.

Overview

The world is constantly shifting beneath the feet of industries and governmental bodies, and foreign direct investment ought to be as diverse as the populations it serves.

The Dieng Group is constantly engaging U.S. industries to invest in Africa, specifically in West African nations. Our consultancy is persistently working to build dynamic relationships with policymakers in Senegal, The Gambia, and Ethiopia, amongst other countries on the African continent.

Therefore, it is vital to cultivate long-term strategies to attract, secure, and maintain high-quality FDI.

Key Capabilities

Strategy Development

Designing data-driven FDI strategies aligned with national priorities, sector strengths, and long-term economic goals.

Sector Studies& Market Intelligence

Deep sector research and competitive benchmarking to identify high-potential industries and attractive investment opportunities.

Economic Growth Strategies

Formulating targeted growth strategies to boost investment inflows, employment generation, and sustainable economic development.

Global Investor Engagement

Organizing trade missions and forums to connect governments with global investors and promote investment opportunities.

Investor & Project Pipeline

Building and managing a strong pipeline of qualified investors and investment-ready projects.

Investment Facilitation Services

End-to-end support for investors, from entry planning to approvals, coordination, and operational setup.

Public-Private Partnerships (PPPs)

Structuring and advising on PPP models to deliver infrastructure and public services efficiently.

Education Partnerships

Enabling public-private collaborations to strengthen education systems, skills development, and workforce readiness.

Transaction Advisory Services

Providing integrated legal, financial, and strategic advisory for complex, multi-stakeholder investment transactions.
