Dr. Stephanie Wanza

Managing Principal | Private Industry & NGOs | The Dieng Group

Professional Overview

Dr. Stephanie Wanza is the Managing Principal of Global Private Industry & Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) sectors at The Dieng Group. She is a seasoned servant leader with more than 27 years of experience across private, non-profit, and public sectors.

Her leadership focuses on advancing policy implementation, compliance, education, scholarship, and organizational transformation. She is widely recognized as a change maker who builds sustainable systems and empowers communities.

In addition to her corporate leadership, Dr. Wanza is a pastor, author, and founder of Fountain of Life International Ministries and Kingdom Seekers Network.

Community Engagement

Dr. Wanza has actively contributed to community development through youth programs, advocacy, and social justice initiatives. She has collaborated with organizations such as NAACP, YMCA, United Way, and the American Cancer Society.

She has led programs focused on voting rights, education, historic preservation, and economic empowerment. Her leadership extends into ministry and community service, advocating for marginalized and underserved communities.
